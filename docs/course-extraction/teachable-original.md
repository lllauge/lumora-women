## Section 1: Postpartum Nutrition Guide

### Introduction (38644181, image lesson)
- "It's easier said than done to tell yourself to lose weight...but let's be realistic, you have a newborn, job maybe, other kids maybe, a partner maybe, and anything else that you could be juggling."
- "But don't worry, I got you covered..."
- Guide broken into **3 EASY phases** on adding nutrient-dense whole foods into your diet without added stress, to lose weight and improve mindset overall.

### Phase 1 (38245883, image lesson) — "add, don't restrict" foods
- **Phase 1-A**: Add ½ cup blueberries or blackberries daily. Add to: yogurt, smoothies, oatmeal/overnight oats, or as a side dish. P.S. no substituting with juice — stay away from juice.
- **Phase 1-B**: Add 1–2 cups dark leafy greens daily. Add to: salads, sandwiches, wraps, omelets, smoothies. P.S. dark leafy greens = kale, spinach, arugula.
- **Phase 1-C**: Add ¼ cup nuts daily. As salad topping, side dish, or snack. P.S. any nut okay; avoid salty and candied nuts.
- **Substitutes**:
  - 1-A: raspberries, goji berries, strawberries, grapes, cherries
  - 1-B: bok choy, lettuce, broccoli, radish greens
  - 1-C: flax seeds, chia seeds, hemp seeds, avocado, almond butter (1 tbsp)
- **Gate**: Do NOT advance to Phase 2 until all 3 foods have been incorporated daily for at least 2 weeks.

### Phase 2 (38245937, image lesson)
- **Phase 2-A**: In addition to Phase 1, add 1–3 tbsp hemp or chia seeds daily. Add to: yogurt, smoothies, oatmeal/overnight oats, soups, salads.
- **Phase 2-B**: Add lean proteins equivalent to your palm daily: chicken breast, salmon, fat-free turkey, egg whites, Greek yogurt, tofu, edamame, tempeh, lentils, chickpeas, beans, shrimp.
- **Phase 2-C**: Add ½–1 cup whole grains daily: quinoa, bulgur, millet, whole wheat bread, buckwheat, oatmeal, farro, brown rice.
- **Substitutes (2-A,B,C combined list)**: legumes, tofu, tempeh, salmon, chicken breast, protein powder, quinoa, egg whites, edamame, haddock, cod, halibut, tilapia, grass-fed beef, bison, low-fat cottage cheese, low-fat Greek yogurt.
- **Gate**: Do NOT advance to Phase 3 until Phase 1 & 2 foods have been incorporated daily for at least 3 weeks.

### Phase 3 (38246018, image lesson)
- Water — "the most important part in losing weight!" Start with a minimum of 2 cups (standard plastic water bottle) daily.
- Eventually the goal is 12 cups of water daily... but start with 2 cups.
- P.S. 1 cup = 8 fl oz.

## Section 2: 6 Week Workout Plan

### Introduction (38646241, text lesson)
- **Disclaimer**: consult physician before starting any workout program; informational use only; author disclaims responsibility for injury.
- **Intro**: At the end of every workout do 5–10 min cardio of your choice (running, jogging, treadmill, biking, etc.).
- Don't rush the exercises — can cause injury. Repeat exercises to get form right; take breaks as needed. "Safety is the most important!"
- Designed for home or gym. 6-week program; can repeat weeks — just increase weight and reps/sets.

### Weekly workouts (3 days/week + 5–10 min cardio finisher each day)

**Week 1 (38565142)**
- Legs – Monday: Squats 3×8-10 · Glute Bridge 3×10-12 · Lying Hip Abductions 3×10-12 each
- Arms – Wednesday: Lying Skull Crushers 3×8-10 · Lateral Raises 3×10-12 · Overhead Tricep Extension 4×10-12
- Legs – Friday: Walking Lunge with Pulse 3×10-12 each · Standing Kickbacks 3×10-12 · Jump Squats 3×10-12

**Week 2 (38565267)**
- Arms – Monday: Anterior Raises 4×8-10 · Lateral Raise 4×12-14 · Overhead Tricep Extensions 4×10-12
- Legs – Wednesday: Jump Squats 4×10-12 · Walking Lunge with Pulse 4×12 each · Plank Kickbacks 5×12 each
- Arms – Friday: Bent Over Dumbbell Rows 5×12-14 · Hammer Curls 5×10 · Bicep Curls 5×12-14

**Week 3 (38565270)**
- Legs – Monday: Plank Kickbacks 4×10-12 each · Jump Squats 4×10-12 · Walking Lunge with Pulse 3×10-12
- Arms – Wednesday: Bent Over Dumbbell Rows 5×10-12 · Hammer Curls 4×12-14 · Bicep Curls 4×12-14
- Legs – Friday: Sumo Squats 4×8-10 · Glute Bridge 5×8-10 · Jump Squats 4×12-14

**Week 4 (38565274)**
- Arms – Monday: Anterior Raises 4×8-10 · Lateral Raises 4×8-10 · Dumbbell Chest Press 4×8-10 each
- Legs – Wednesday: Sumo Squats 4×8-10 · Walking Lunge with Pulse 4×8-10 · Plank Kickbacks 4×8-10 each
- Arms – Friday: Bent Over Dumbbell Rows 4×8-10 · Hammer Curls 4×10-12 each · Bicep Curls 4×12-14

**Week 5 (38565275)**
- Legs – Monday: Glute Bridge 4×10-12 · Squat Jumps 3×10-12 · Walking Lunge with Pulse 4×15 each
- Arms – Wednesday: Overhead Tricep Extension 4×10-12 · Lying Skull Crushers 3×8-10 · Dumbbell Chest Press 4×12-14
- Legs – Friday: Glute Bridge 4×8-10 · Sumo Squats 3×12-14 · Plank Kickbacks 4×12-14

**Week 6 (38565277)**
- Arms – Monday: Anterior Raises 4×8-10 · Lateral Raise 4×12-14 · Overhead Tricep Extensions 4×10-12
- Legs – Wednesday: Jump Squats 4×10-12 · Walking Lunge with Pulse 4×12 each · Plank Kickbacks 5×12 each
- Arms – Friday: Bent Over Dumbbell Rows 5×12-14 · Hammer Curls 5×10 · Bicep Curls 5×12-14
- (Week 6 repeats Week 2's plan.)

## Observations for the rebuild (my notes, not original content)
- No timeline gating relative to birth/medical clearance beyond the generic disclaimer; no pelvic floor / diastasis recti screening; no core/breathing rehab phase.
- Jump squats appear as early as Week 1 — high-impact work very early for a postpartum population; needs evidence review.
- No mention of breastfeeding calorie/hydration needs beyond generic water advice; "fat-free turkey" / "low-fat" framing is dated diet-culture language.
- Nutrition phases are habit-based additions (berries, greens, nuts, seeds, protein, grains, water) — good behavior-change bones worth keeping.
- Original branding: green/cream nutrition cards, purple workout headers, Century-Gothic-style headings.
